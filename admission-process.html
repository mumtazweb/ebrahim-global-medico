ADMISSION PROCESS

Step-by-Step MBBS Abroad Admission Support

From free counselling to hostel coordination, our process keeps students and parents informed.

1

Free Counselling

Discuss NEET status, budget, academic profile and preferred country.

2

Document Check

Review passport, academic marksheets, NEET details and identity documents.

3

Country & University Selection

Compare options based on eligibility, fee range, medium and student goals.

4

Offer Letter

Submit the application and coordinate for the university offer letter.

5

Admission Confirmation

Complete admission formalities as per university instructions.

6

Visa Processing

Prepare visa documents and guide the student through the visa process.

7

Travel & Hostel Support

Coordinate travel readiness, arrival guidance and hostel support.

8

First-Year Check-in

Follow up after arrival so students understand class start, hostel routines and early academic steps.

Required Documents

  • 10th marksheet and certificate
  • 12th marksheet and certificate
  • NEET scorecard and admit card
  • Valid passport
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Aadhaar card or identity proof
  • Medical fitness documents if required
