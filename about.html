About Global Medico Admissions

Your Dream, Our Guidance. We help students and parents make informed MBBS Abroad decisions.

OUR STORY

Premium Counselling for Medical Aspirants

Global Medico Admissions provides structured guidance for students planning to study MBBS Abroad. Our counselling focuses on eligibility, budget, university recognition, documentation, visa readiness, hostel support and long-term academic planning.

We keep the process practical and parent-friendly, so families understand what they are choosing before making a commitment.

Counselling Approach

  • Student profile review
  • Country and university comparison
  • Transparent fee and document discussion
  • Visa and travel coordination support
  • FMGE/NExT-oriented academic planning

Mission

To make MBBS Abroad counselling clear, reliable and accessible for students who need the right guidance at the right time.

Vision

To become a trusted medical education guidance partner for Indian students seeking global opportunities.

Why Students Trust Us

We combine practical counselling, document discipline and family communication throughout the admission journey.
